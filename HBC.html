Haunting Beautiful Cosmetics

A cosmetics line for creatures of the night.

Date: Dec 9, 2022 & April 21, 2023

Title: Haunting Beautiful Cosmetics Brochure and Products

Job Titles: Illustrator, Copywriter, Graphic Designer

Software/hardware: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Summary

This brand and line of products are the result of two different assignments. The focus on one assignment was precise and concise writing featuring products or services that didn't exist. I used my skills in Photoshop and Illustrator to stylize the brochure for a novel cosmetic company. I used this project as inspiration for my Shopify assignment that I had in Content Management Systems. I made two additional products, a lip gloss and a toothpaste. I wanted to have an elegant but a dark look. I took inspiration for the logo and color pallet from old wine, perfume and potion labels. The content is inspired by historical horror movies. Filled with puns, the text adds a fun and humorous side without taking away from the sleek look of the document.

Logo and Design

The project started with the idea of moonscreen, which would be applied like sunscreen. The concepts that helped shape the logo design are the words “luxury” and “premium”. I used rich colors like red, black and gold to sell the idea that this was a product that those of high class would use. Black is a color that is often associated with night and I used it as the foundation to apply the other elements. The deep red compliments the darkness that comes from the black. The gold adds a contrasting highlight as well as to communicate the idea of it being that luxury item. The shape with the die cut corners was directly inspired by the labels. It also gives the logo a b shape that is neither feminine or masculine. The bats were initially hand drawn and were created to push the narrative of the “creature of the night” and add an element of elegance with the fine detail. The drawings were then translated into vectors to make sure that all of the symmetry lined up.

Copy

To create the copy for the brochure, I drew inspiration from a cast of classic horror story and film characters, infusing the text with a blend of puns and satire. The catalyst for this creative process was the enigmatic aristocrat Dorian Gray, as imagined by both Oscar Wilde and Alan Moore. Mr. Gray's review, 'I can't see myself without it,' sets the tone for the brochure's whimsical yet eerie atmosphere. In both Wilde's 'The Picture of Dorian Gray' and Moore's portrayal, Dorian hides away his portrait, a private reflection of his moral degradation, concealed from prying eyes within his home.

Then there are the doctor recommendations. Dr. Victor Frankenstein is my favorite of the recommendations. He goes to say “Each ingredient has been patiently and expertly collected and combined in a way that brings new life to the beauty and skin care industry.” This highlights the base of the story of Frankenstein by Mary Shelly in which Dr. Frankenstein creates a new life from collected body parts. I had a lot of fun trying to find all of the ways I could play with words.

Products

The initial product is the moonscreen called Moon Scream and the two products that were created for my Shopify assignment are a line of lip glosses based on poisonous flowers, Good Night Kiss, and fang paste, Pearly Bites. I continued to use the same base logo shape as the Moon Scream but on the corners the images were changed to relate to the new products. The colors that were used are black and gold with the main color related to the variation on the product.

Bonus

It hasn’t been put up for sale yet, but I did make a repeating pattern to use for fabrics and wallpaper using the bats. I used Adobe Illustrator to create a seamless pattern in two different size variations. I intend on getting some fabric printed to make a dress out of and I know someone who wants to order it as wallpaper for her dining room.
