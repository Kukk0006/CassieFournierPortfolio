Tablet Weaving

Project Info:

Embarking on the journey of mastering tablet weaving, an ancient art dating back to the 8th century BCE, has been a deeply enriching experience for me. Unlike traditional weaving methods, tablet weaving intricately twists warp threads around themselves, resulting in durable, braided-like bands.

By incorporating tablet-woven bands into everyday items like belts and dog collars, I've witnessed firsthand the remarkable durability and versatility of this craft. Furthermore, these tangible examples serve as a testament to the lasting legacy of tablet weaving, showcasing its ability to seamlessly blend tradition with modern functionality.

My journey with tablet weaving exemplifies the timeless allure and practicality of this ancient craft. Through experimentation and application, I've come to appreciate not only its historical significance but also its enduring relevance in today's world. From ancient origins to modern applications, tablet weaving continues to weave its way into the fabric of my creative pursuits.
