The Beauty of Japan

Project Info:

I created a personal project to practice the skills I learned in the LinkedIn course "Premiere Pro for Social Media Content" by Nick Harauz. I curated stock imagery and sourced music from YouTube's library, remixing it using Premiere Pro's built-in tools with manual adjustments. My focus was on cutting to the music, keeping the post concise, and creating a smooth flow from one scene to the next through related details. This project allowed me to refine my editing techniques and enhance my ability to produce engaging social media content.
