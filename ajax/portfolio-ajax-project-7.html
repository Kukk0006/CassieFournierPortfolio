Sassy Cat Studios

Project Info:

The branding I developed initially in high school and refined through levels one and two of the Interactive Media Design program holds significant personal meaning to me. If I were to open my own studio, this is the branding I would choose to represent it. The faceless cat logo has been a constant symbol in my life for nearly 20 years, and its enduring presence speaks volumes about its resonance and significance to me.

Over the years, I have worked to blend the old with the new, merging nostalgic elements with modern design principles. This journey has allowed me to evolve the branding while maintaining its core identity. The faceless cat embodies a sense of timelessness and adaptability, qualities that I believe are essential for any successful brand. By integrating these aspects, I have created a unique and cohesive visual identity that reflects both my personal history and my professional growth.

My goal is to carry forward this blend of tradition and innovation into my future endeavors, using it as a foundation for my studio's brand. I am confident that this well-balanced and meaningful branding will resonate with clients and audiences alike, just as it has with me over the past two decades.
