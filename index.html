About Me

Know Me More

I'm Cassie Fournier, a Interactive Media Designer

I'm a recent graduate from Algonquin College of Applied Arts and Technology. My college ride has been quite the adventure, exploring a mix of subjects—from web design and photography to videography and UX design. But you know what really caught my eye? Motion graphics! During a group project in the video production course, I took charge of creating most of the motion graphics used. That experience got me hyped up for an upcoming dedicated course in that field. Exploring motion graphics got me hooked because it's just so fascinating and has a combination of creativity and logic!

I'm all about soaking up new knowledge—I dive into tutorials across a wide range of topics. My only setback is sometimes not fully mastering a specific subject. That's also where my strength lies—I've got a solid grasp of many topics and an insatiable appetite to learn more. This inherent curiosity keeps me driven to expand my skill set and venture into uncharted territories. Here's to embracing the joy of learning across the board!
