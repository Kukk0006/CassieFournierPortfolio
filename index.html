About Me

About Me

I'm a recent graduate from Algonquin College of Applied Arts and Technology. My college ride has been quite the adventure, exploring a mix of subjects—from web design and photography to videography and UX design. But you know what really caught my eye? Motion graphics! During a group project in the video production course, I took charge of creating most of the motion graphics used. That experience got me hyped up for an upcoming dedicated course in that field. Exploring motion graphics got me hooked because it's just so fascinating and has a combination of creativity and logic!

I'm all about soaking up new knowledge—I dive into tutorials across a wide range of topics. My only setback is sometimes not fully mastering a specific subject. That's also where my strength lies—I've got a solid grasp of many topics and an insatiable appetite to learn more. This inherent curiosity keeps me driven to expand my skill set and venture into uncharted territories. Here's to embracing the joy of learning across the board!

Video and Motion Design

I found a strong enjoyment in video editing, animation and motion design throughout my studies. It is definitely one area I am looking to expand and deepen my knowledge and skills. Here is a collection of projects that I have completed playing a key role in those positions.

From Passion to Pint: Brewer Matt Tweedy's Journey to Mastery

This was a group project in level three. We did a biopic of a local brewer and brewpub owner of Tooth and Nail in the heart of the Hintonburg in Ontario.

My roles were: Camera Operator, Motion Graphic Designer, Editor, Lighting/Gaffer

AllerCheck Connect

A group assignment in level four that a next gen technology was to be conceptualized that required two devices to talk to each other and feature a distinct UI interaction and make a promo video for it.

My roles were: Storyboards, Prop Designer, Motion Graphic Designer for the device, Assistant Director & Creative Director

Timeless Drift

A group assignment in level two. We were to make a creative video implementing all the steps of video production.

My roles were: Camera Operator, Video Editing, Motion Graphic Designer

Illustration and Branding
